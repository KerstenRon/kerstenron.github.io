About the Designer

Alex is a game designer focused on level design, systemic mechanics, and clearly communicated player goals. Projects range from compact prototypes to vertical slices with fully playable builds.

The core interest is readable level flow, iterative prototyping, and making design decisions backed by playtest data.

Key Skills

  • Level & encounter design
  • Systems & economy design
  • Rapid prototyping (Unity, Unreal)
  • Documentation & pitch decks

Featured Projects

Ghostlines

Isometric tactics-puzzle prototype about ghost trains and line of sight.

Role

Core game design, level design, enemy archetype balancing, paper prototyping.

Design Goals

  • Clear line-of-sight rules and stealth readability.
  • Short levels with a strong “one more try” pull.
  • Minimal UI, feedback mostly through animation and VFX.

Skybound City

Cozy city builder on floating islands with system-driven gameplay.

Role

Systems design, economy design, UX flows, telemetry review from playtests.

Design Goals

  • Interlocking production chains without overwhelming new players.
  • Smooth progression driven by soft gates instead of hard walls.
  • Emotional attachment via resident stories and micro-events.

Bossloop

Roguelite boss rush that treats every attempt as a learning loop.

Role

Combat design, boss patterns, input feel, difficulty curves, onboarding.

Design Goals

  • “Fair but tough” difficulty with highly readable attacks.
  • Short runs with deep meta progression.
  • Strong game feel for dodge, parry, and special moves.
